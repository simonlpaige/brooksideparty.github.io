# PHOTO-STYLE-GUIDE.md - Brookside Party Warehouse

## Image Treatment Rules

- All product photos: warm, bright natural light. Avoid harsh flash.
- Store atmosphere shots: capture the party vibe - colorful, festive, welcoming.
- Halloween/Spookier season: darker mood OK, but keep a sense of fun (not scary/gory).
- Aspect ratios: square (1:1) for products, 16:9 or 4:3 for hero/atmosphere shots.
- File format: JPEG for photos (quality 85%), WebP preferred for performance.
- Max size: 2MB per image before deploy. Compress with squoosh.app or tinypng.com.

## Alt Text Rules (mandatory)

Every `<img>` needs a meaningful alt attribute. Examples:
- `alt="Balloon arch in red, orange, and yellow"` (descriptive, not "photo of balloons")
- `alt="Halloween costumes display in store window"`
- `alt="Brookside Party Warehouse storefront"`
- Decorative images: `alt=""` (empty, not missing)

## Naming Convention

- `img-01.jpg`, `img-02.jpg` etc. for general store photos
- `spook-01.jpg` etc. for Halloween-specific
- `product-[name].jpg` for specific products
